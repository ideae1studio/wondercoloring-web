African Lion

Panthera leo

Key Characteristics

  • They are the only cats that live in groups called prides.
  • Male lions have manes, while females do not.

Peculiar Facts (Fun Facts!)

  • A lion's roar can be heard up to 5 miles (8 km) away.
  • They spend most of the day sleeping or resting (up to 20 hours).
