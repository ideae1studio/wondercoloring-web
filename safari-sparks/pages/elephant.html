Elephant

African Bush Elephant

Key Characteristics

  • TThe African Elephant is the largest land animal on Earth, weighing as much as three cars!
  • Their long trunk is like a super-powered hand: they use it to drink, breathe (snorkel!), shower, and pick up tiny food.
  • Elephant herds are led by the oldest and wisest female, called the 'Matriarch'.

Peculiar Facts (Fun Facts!)

  • An elephant's ears are HUGE! They flap them like fans to cool down their body in the hot African climate.
  • They can communicate over long distances using low rumbling sounds that are too low for humans to hear.
  • Elephants are highly emotional and have been observed mourning their dead loved ones.
