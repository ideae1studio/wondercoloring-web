Meerkat

Suricata suricatta

Key Characteristics

  • Meerkats live in groups called 'mobs' or 'gangs' and work together to survive.
  • They take turns standing tall as a 'sentry' or lookout to watch for danger, like eagles.
  • They teach their babies how to hunt scorpions and other creepy crawlies safely.

Peculiar Facts (Fun Facts!)

  • When they get cold, meerkats sunbathe! They stand on their hind legs and face the sun to warm up their tummies.
  • Their black eye patches act like sunglasses, helping them see clearly in the bright desert sun.
  • A meerkat den can have many entrances and tunnels, almost like an underground castle!
