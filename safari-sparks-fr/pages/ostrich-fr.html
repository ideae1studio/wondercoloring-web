Ostrich

Struthio camelus

Key Characteristics

  • The Ostrich is the world's largest and heaviest bird, but it cannot fly!
  • Instead of flying, they are amazing runners, using their large, powerful legs to escape danger.
  • They have only two toes on each foot‚Äîperfect for high-speed running on the African plains.
